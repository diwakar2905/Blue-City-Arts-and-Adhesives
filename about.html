Our Story

A legacy of Rajasthani craftsmanship merged with modern manufacturing excellence.

Heritage and Excellence

Blue City Arts and Adhesives began with a profound mission: to elevate the rich heritage of Rajasthani artistry to the global stage.

Over our decorated history, we have exponentially expanded our core capabilities from handcrafted wooden furniture to comprehensive turnkey solutions and high-grade industrial adhesives.

Our masterful artisans work seamlessly alongside advanced machinery, ensuring every single product is not just an item of functionality, but a true masterpiece designed to last generations.

The Pillars of Our Success

What drives Blue City to deliver unparalleled excellence across borders.

Uncompromising Quality

We carefully select only the finest grade raw materials, from seasoned Rajasthani teakwood to industrial strength chemical compounds, ensuring absolute durability.

Modern Innovation

While our roots are deeply traditional, our factory floor is powered by cutting-edge European machinery to guarantee precision scaling and rapid turnaround times.

Heritage Craftsmanship

As a brand birthed in Jodhpur, 'The Blue City', our soul lies securely in the intricate joinery, block carving, and meticulous hand-polishing techniques native to local artisans.

Our Vision

To be the globally preferred partner for architects, interior designers, and bespoke furniture retailers by seamlessly bridging traditional Indian handicraft with ultra-modern manufacturing precision.

Our Mission

To passionately manufacture and export masterfully crafted furniture and robust adhesive solutions without a single compromise on sustainability, visual design, or structural integrity.

Blue City Arts and Adhesives

Crafted in Rajasthan. Delivered Worldwide.

Blue City Arts and Adhesives is a Rajasthan-based manufacturing company specializing in premium furniture, handicrafts, and industrial adhesive solutions. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern manufacturing to deliver high-quality products and turnkey project solutions across India and internationally.
